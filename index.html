My 3 years of experience as a packaging graphic designer at Altavia Benelux taught me a lot about the technical and creative aspects of print graphic design.

Web banners, infographics, social media imagery and presentations are a big part of my daily life as a senior graphic designer in a corporate communication agency.

MOTION

I've had several occasions to work on explainer videos or video editing, which I'm doing with After Effects. I'm eager to continue learning this software!

WEBDESIGN

Newsletters and landing pages design are the latest additions to my realisations. I also have some basic knowledge of HTML and CSS I'd like to deepen.
