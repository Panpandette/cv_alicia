Packaging was my first real interest in the field of graphic design. You can scroll a bit to see some works I did as a graphic designer at Altavia Benelux, for clients as retailers Carrefour, Dia and Auchan.

Belgian Beer

packaging

Here are some proposals for a Belgian beer that's all about a local celebration in the city of Mons called "the Doudou". It's celebrating Saint Georges killing a green and red dragon to protect the inhabitants. "Chambourlette" is a specific term designating people who go and celebrate with the Montois, even if they're not from that region.
